📊 Everyone Delivers Data. We Deliver Context.

After every session, Race Mind analyzes your training – not just numbers, but what they mean for you. Pace, heart rate, cadence, elevation: Everything is put in context of your current fitness and your goal.

The result isn’t a dashboard full of charts, but a clear assessment you understand immediately – no sports science degree required.

  • What went well? Not just green numbers, but an explanation of why the session worked – and what you can take away from it.
  • What was suboptimal? Specific pointers on what to do differently next time – no vague “could be better”.
  • What’s the focus? One clear takeaway for your next session – no guesswork, no overload.

🏋️ Your Plan. Not Off the Shelf.

Race Mind doesn’t build your training plan from a template. Every week is individually calculated – based on your goal, your availability, your current fitness, and your training history.

Race Mind draws from a workout library with over 300 sessions: swimming, cycling, running, strength, mobility, and stability – tailored to your sport, training phase, and performance level.

  • Phase-appropriate: Base, Build, Peak, Taper – each phase has its own priorities and intensities.
  • Sport-specific: Run intervals, threshold rides, technique swims, functional strength training – all from one library.
  • Individually assembled: Which sessions land in your week depends on your goals, your load, and your available time.
  • With guidance: Every workout explains its purpose, execution, and intensity – so you know why you’re doing what you’re doing.

💬 Ask Your Coach

Got a question at 10 PM? Knee acting up before race day? Not sure if you should jump back in after a cold? The coach is always there – and knows your plan, your data, and your history.

Not a generic ChatGPT, but a coach who knows what you trained last week and what’s coming up next.

  • Plan changes on the fly: “Can I move the intervals to tomorrow?” – the coach adjusts your plan without breaking the weekly structure.
  • Comeback after a break: Been sick, on vacation, or just not feeling it – the coach builds you a realistic path back.
  • Nutrition & race day: What before the long run, what during the race? Concrete recommendations instead of Google rabbit holes.

🎯 Race Time Prediction

From day one, Race Mind calculates a realistic target time – either from imported training history or from your self-assessment during onboarding. You don’t need existing data to get started.

This prediction isn’t a fixed number: It gets more accurate every week and shows you how your performance is developing. So you can tell early whether you’re on track – or whether you should course-correct with the coach before it’s too late.

  • Prediction from day 1: Whether with data or self-assessment – you immediately see where you stand and what’s realistically possible.
  • Track your progress: Your prediction updates continuously. You see whether the trend is right or if you need to adjust.
  • Stay in control: When the curve dips, ask the coach – and get concrete recommendations to get back on track.

Frequently Asked Questions

Is this a static plan or does it really adapt?

Race Mind adjusts your weekly plan when your schedule, availability, or recovery changes. You don’t need to “catch up” – you get a week that makes sense again.

What data do I need for it to work?

A wearable helps but isn’t required. The more data (heart rate, pace/power, sleep), the more accurate the prediction and feedback become.

What goals is Race Mind designed for?

For Ironman/70.3 and running events like marathon and half marathon – always focused on target time and race-specific build-up.
