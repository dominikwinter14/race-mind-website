Frequently Asked Questions

“I already have a Garmin/Coros. Do I need Race Mind?”

Not necessarily – but that’s exactly where Race Mind shines. Your existing data isn’t just displayed, it’s translated into a plan, a race time prediction, and concrete decisions for your week. You don’t have to start from scratch or transfer data manually. If you already train with Garmin, COROS, Strava, or intervals.icu, Race Mind plugs right in.

“What happens if I can’t train at all for a week?”

You don’t have to make up for anything. Race Mind can shift, adjust, or skip sessions without your week falling apart. When a single tweak isn’t enough, the week gets replanned instead of just pushing everything back. The goal isn’t perfection – it’s a plan that still makes sense when real life gets in the way.

“How do you make sure the AI doesn’t push me into an injury?”

Race Mind doesn’t just assess what you could handle – it also considers what makes sense right now. Sleep, HRV, resting heart rate, and training load all factor in, so a hard day can consciously become an easy day or a rest day. Bigger adjustments aren’t blindly crammed into your week but replanned in a controlled way. That said: Race Mind is a training coach, not a substitute for medical or physiotherapy advice.

“Does Race Mind replace a human coach?”

No. A good human coach can see and assess things that no app can fully replace. But Race Mind handles many of the tasks that really matter day-to-day: a plan that adapts, clear explanations instead of metric overload, and quick help when a week doesn’t go as planned. For many, that’s far more helpful than a static plan – and far more accessible than 1:1 coaching.

“What sets you apart from TrainingPeaks/TriDot/Humango?”

Race Mind combines three things that are often separate: adaptation, explainability, and direct action. Instead of just showing data, the coach explains what it means for your training. Instead of just giving recommendations, Race Mind can move sessions or replan your week directly. And instead of rigidly sticking to a plan, the focus stays on what’s realistic and doable in your life right now.

“How does cycle tracking work – and do I have to use it?”

You don’t have to. If you enable it, Race Mind can factor your cycle into load and planning decisions. Depending on your preference, the plan can continue normally, become slightly more cautious, or protect period days more. It’s not a miracle promise – it’s an optional adjustment feature for more realism in your training.

“I’m a first-timer. Is Race Mind too advanced for me?”

No. You can start with imported data, but also with self-assessment and manual fitness input. Especially as a first-timer, what matters is getting a realistic race time prediction, understanding why you’re training what you’re training, and being able to ask whenever something is unclear. That’s exactly what Race Mind is built for.

“Can I cancel or pause my plan if I stop training?”

Yes, you can cancel anytime. Cancellation is handled through the subscription management in the App Store or Google Play. If you’re not training right now, you don’t have to stay locked into a running subscription. And if you want to delete your account entirely, that’s possible too.
