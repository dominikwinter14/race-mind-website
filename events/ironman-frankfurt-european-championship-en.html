Course description

The German classic and European Championship with a skyline finish at the Römer. The swim is in the Langener Waldsee (with an Australian exit between laps), and the rolling two-loop bike rolls well but features punchy Taunus climbs ('The Beast'). The run is flat along the Main riverbank.

Coach tip

  • In the heat, the shadeless run along the Main becomes brutal — plan a consistent cooling strategy and drink early. On the bike, ride the rolling Taunus climbs evenly and don't over-pace them. Use the Australian exit during the swim for orientation and to settle into a calm rhythm.
