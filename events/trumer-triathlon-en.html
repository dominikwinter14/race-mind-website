Course description

A middle-distance triathlon in the Salzburger Seenland. The swim is in a scenic bay of Lake Obertrum, followed by an undulating, demanding bike course through the hilly lake district. The run leads through the centre of Obertrum with a welcoming, family atmosphere.

Coach tip

  • The undulating, demanding bike course requires good effort management: ride the climbs in a controlled way to power and don't over-pace them. The race is stunningly scenic with a welcoming, family atmosphere. Use the flat run in the centre for an even, steady rhythm.
