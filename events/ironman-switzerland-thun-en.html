Course description

A course in Thun with stunning mountain panorama but challenging climbs. The swim is in the often-cool Lake Thun, followed by a two-loop bike through the Gürbetal and Gantrisch region with around 2,000 metres of climbing. The lakeside run offers views of the Eiger, Mönch and Jungfrau.

Coach tip

  • The water in Lake Thun is often cool (around 17°C) — test your wetsuit. The bike demands strong climbing legs: its roughly 2,000 metres of climbing should be ridden evenly and to power. On the lakeside run, the unique panorama of the Eiger, Mönch and Jungfrau carries you to the finish.
