Course description

A half-distance race on the Dorset coast with a swim in the cold, often rough sea. The rolling single-loop bike runs through the English countryside around Dorchester, while the flat run follows the scenic seafront esplanade. Beautiful scenery, but typically changeable English weather.

Coach tip

  • Prepare for cold water — a wetsuit is mandatory and face protection helps for the sea swim. The rolling bike is typically English: ride evenly and ration your strength. Plan for wind and rain; in severe weather the swim can be shortened or cancelled, so stay flexible with your race plan.
