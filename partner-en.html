Frequently asked questions

"When do I get my commission?"

Commissions are settled monthly. There's a 60-day hold period: commissions are released after that to cover refunds and chargebacks. Payout via SEPA once you reach €50 in your partner account.

"How do you track who discovered RaceMind through me?"

You use your personal friend code that you already have in the app (e.g. lisa1234). Just share your link race-mind.com/friend/<your-code>. Anyone who installs and subscribes to Premium through it gets automatically attributed to you.

"What counts as a conversion?"

A new Premium user who, within 30 days of installing the app, subscribes to Premium using your code. Commission is paid for 12 months from the initial subscription, as long as the subscription remains active.

"What happens if a user cancels or gets a refund?"

If a user cancels within the first 60 days or receives a refund from the App Store or Google Play, the corresponding commission won't be paid out or will be deducted. After 60 days, commissions are final.

"Do I need to pay taxes on the commission?"

Yes. Commission income is taxable – you are responsible for declaring it correctly. For payouts we need your name, address, and tax ID. Above certain thresholds (DAC7) we are legally required to report your data to the tax authorities.

"Do I have to label my recommendations as advertising?"

Yes. Once you receive compensation, the recommendation must be labeled (UWG § 5a Abs. 4 in Germany, similar rules elsewhere). Use clear labels like #ad or #partner in posts, stories, or videos.

"Can I recommend RaceMind even if I'm not a Premium user?"

You don't have to be a Premium user yourself, but we recommend it: you can only authentically recommend what you actually use. On approval, you can request a complimentary Premium access.

"What about self-referrals?"

Referrals to yourself (same person, same payment method, same device) don't count. In case of repeated abuse we reserve the right to close the partner account and withhold outstanding commissions.

"When does the program start?"

We're launching in a pilot phase with hand-picked partners. Applications from DACH (Germany, Austria, Switzerland) are accepted from now on. International partners follow later.
